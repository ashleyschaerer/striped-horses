About us

"I was undiagnosed between the age of 17 and 21. Looking back at that time and remembering how helpless my parents felt led me to start this community. It's hard enough to see your child being in pain, but not knowing why and how to get your child  better is another level of suffering. Going through such a time is often isolating and taxing on your mental health. It's so important to have a strong support system and that's what this app is all about — creating a community of moms that are there for each other, no matter if you're looking for advice on doctor appointments or seeking someone to support you emotionally."
— Ashley Schaerer, Co-founder

"You never realize the amount of magic your mom had to create to shield you from the bad things in life when you were a child.   It wasn't until recently when I started to comb through stories of my youth that I realized most of my happiest moments were some of the most challenging times for her.  Who I am today is a result of all the moments she painstakingly worked so hard to create. I hope that by helping build this community, we can help moms around the world do the same for their children while also making life  a little less difficult."
— Kingsley Wong, Co-founder
